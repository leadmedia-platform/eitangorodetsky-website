# Frameworks

## The AI-native maturity ladder
Tools → Assistants → Colleagues → Operating system. A model for how deeply a
marketing operation runs *on* AI rather than merely *using* it.
https://eitangorodetsky.com/writing/ai-native-marketing-operation

## The compounding test
Separates AI leverage from AI theatre: does it touch the real system, remember
across time, and run repeatedly + improve?
https://eitangorodetsky.com/writing/where-ai-moves-roi

## Govern the blast radius, not the intelligence
AI governance as an accelerator — least-privilege hands, gated promotion,
provenance, data boundaries, named accountability. Control what AI can touch,
not what it can think.
https://eitangorodetsky.com/writing/ai-governance-blast-radius
